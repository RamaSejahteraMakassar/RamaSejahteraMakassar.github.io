Decades of Excellence

Founded in 1995 by Dr. Rama Sejahtera, our foundation began with a simple dream: to provide high-quality education that remains accessible to the community. What started as a small primary school with 40 students has flourished into a comprehensive educational complex.

In 2010, the foundation achieved national accreditation "A" and pioneered the "Academic Excellence Curriculum," which focuses on emotional intelligence and professional ethics. Today, we are home to over 1,500 students from various backgrounds, all striving for excellence.
